5. Atributo `Secure` do cookie (login com `cookie-secure=true`).
6. Chain custom do consumidor (`addFilterBefore`) integra corretamente.
7. Logout (2.3) e logoutAll (2.6).
8. Throttle de `last_accessed_at` (2.2).
